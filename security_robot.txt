In a futuristic robot factory, a charging robot manages battery modules represented as a sequence of states, denoted by the characters D and E. The robot can perform two types of conversions to upgrade the batteries and gain energy units:
1. Find a substring DE and convert it into EC, gaining one energy unit.
2. Find a substring ED and convert it into CE, gaining one energy unit.
Your goal is to determine the maximum number of energy units the robot can collect by performing these conversions on the battery modules.
